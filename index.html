Crystal game!

Can you match the target score with the crystals?
Don't go over!

You will be shown a randomized target number at the start of the game.

You have four crystals. Each one carries a certain value. By clicking a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game if you can get your total score to match the target number.

The value of crystals will change with every game!
